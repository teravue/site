TeraVue Features

TeraVue enables you to conduct full-sized remote sensing and digital cartography projects at an affordable price. With its comprehensive range of tools , it fits the needs of the scientist, the teacher and the commercial user. Designed to run under Windows, it is easy to use and powerful in the amount of data which it can access and process.

Keys features illustrated

 Interactive image processing and data integration

Multispectral remote sensing

Geographic referencing and vector data handling

Hardware requirements

TeraVue runs on 386486 PC to Pentium processors under Windows 3.1 to Windows95/NT, with at least 8 Megabytes of RAM. Program and associated tutorial data files require a minimum of 10 Megabytes of free disk space.

Supported graphics modes include 16 color VGA to upper extended SVGA modes in 8 bits and 24 bits or equivalent. A CD-Rom drive is recommended.
