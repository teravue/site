Image rectification and vector data bridge

  • Geolocation of images on a world atlas at any scale
  • Geometric correction performed either on images or maps through first and second-order polynomial model
  • Least squares fit to ground control points (GCP). Nearest-neighbour resampling.
  • Raster to vector conversion of classified images to DXF format
  • Overlay any image display with DXF files from other systems. Import/Export to AutoCad, ArcInfo ... etc.
